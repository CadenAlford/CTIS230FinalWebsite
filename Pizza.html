Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

  • Lavash Bread
  • Light Ranch
  • Fat Free Mozzarella
  • Frank's Buffalo Sauce
  • Frozen Grilled Chicken

Preparation

First, you want to thaw you grilled chicken. Once thawed, cut it up into small pieces. Begin heating up Buffalo sauce in a pan. In the meantime, spread your ranch on the lavash bread. then toss your chicken pieces in the buffalo sauce. Once Completely coated with sauce, put the Buffalo chicken on top of the lavash bread and then cover with cheese.

Cooking

The cooking process is super simple. After preparing your pizza. Bake them in the oven at 350 degrees fahrenheit. They are finished when the edges are crispy and the cheese is melted.
