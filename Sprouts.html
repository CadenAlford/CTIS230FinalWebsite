Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • Nature's Seasoning
  • Balasmic Vinegar
  • Avocado Oil

Preparation

First, make sure to wash your sprouts in cold salty water. Then, chop off the bottoms and cut each in half. Next, coat your brussels sprouts in Avocado oil and then season with Nature's Seasoning.

Cooking

After Seasoning, place your sprouts onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake your sprouts at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 30-40 minutes. After cooking, the last step is to place your brussels sprouts into a bowl and toss them with Balsamic Vinegar. Enjoy!
