Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenderloins
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avocado Oil
  • Frank's Buffalo Sauce

Preparation

First, you want to season your chicken with the seasonings listed in the ingredients. Then, heat 2 Tbsp of Avocado oil in a pan.

Cooking

After heating up your pan, cooking you tenderloins until they almost done. Heat up a pan with your buffalo sauce in it. When the sauce begins to simmer, toss in your tenderloins. Then, toss your tenderloins in the sauce allow them to cook for a minute or so. Finally, enjoy with a condiment like ranch!
