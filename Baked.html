Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • Pink Himalayan Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

Preparation

Season both sides of the chicken genoursly with all the spices listed in the ingredients. Place seasoned chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchement paper.

Cooking

After all of your chicken in on the baking sheet, bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes on 375 degrees fahrenheit. Flip your chicken halfway through cooking. Enjoy!
